Biography

Data scientist with a PhD in mathematics and industry experience in machine learning engineering. Proven ability to translate complex mathematical concepts into actionable business insights. Experience in developing end-to-end solutions using Python and AWS to solve time series forecasting problems.

Previously worked in academia, where my research was focused on fractal geometry, dynamical systems, and hyperbolic geometry.

My PhD was supervised by Professor Jonathan Fraser and Professor Kenneth Falconer, and was funded by the University of St Andrews.
